PARFAIT

A delicious and visually appealing layered dessert consisting of yogurt, granola, and fruits such as berries, served in a glass.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Granola
  • Mixed berries
  • Honey or maple syrup
  • For extra HP: sliced bananas, sliced almonds, chia seeds

Steps

  1. Arm yourself with a parfait glass or bowl and layer the ingredients with precision and flair: a generous spoonful of Greek yogurt, a sprinkling of crunchy granola, a colorful array of mixed berries, and a drizzle of sweet honey or maple syrup.
  2. Conquer the heights of flavor by repeating the layering process, creating a symphony of tastes and textures that will awaken your taste buds to new culinary horizons.
  3. Brave the unknown as you experiment with optional toppings such as sliced bananas for added creaminess, sliced almonds for a satisfying crunch, and chia seeds for a nutritional boost.
  4. Embark on a journey of indulgence as you dive into your towering parfait creation, allowing the flavors to mingle and dance on your palate in a harmonious celebration of sweetness and crunch. Adventure awaits in every spoonful!
