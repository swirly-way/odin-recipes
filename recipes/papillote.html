PAPILLOTE

Embark on a culinary journey with these rustic potatoes and onions, cooked to perfection in a parchment packet for a delicious campfire feast!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Parchment paper

Steps

  1. Forage for some fresh potatoes, onions, and garlic in the wilderness.
  2. Build a campfire and let it burn down to hot coals.
  3. Slice the potatoes, onions, and garlic with your trusty adventurer's knife.
  4. In a parchment paper packet combine the sliced veggies with olive oil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  5. Seal the packet tightly and place it on a flat rock near the campfire.
  6. Cook the papillote over the hot coals for about 30 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
